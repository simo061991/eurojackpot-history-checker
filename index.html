What is Eurojackpot?

Eurojackpot is a transnational European lottery launched in March 2012. Players select five main numbers from 1 to 50 and two additional "Euro numbers" from 1 to 12. Draws take place every Tuesday and Friday.

Winning Criteria

The minimum winning combination is matching 2 main numbers and 1 Euro number. The more numbers you match, the higher the prize tier you qualify for.
